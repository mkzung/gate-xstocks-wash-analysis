What the data says

  1. The wash is pervasive. Scoring all 31 markets on three signals (clip dominance, circularity, Benford break), 9 of the 13 liquid markets are flagged 0.83-0.99, above three organic controls (MSTRX 0.17, liquid SOL 0.69, LINK 0.43); three more are ambiguous. 18 markets were too thin to score.
  2. Circular volume. The flagged markets run one fixed size that is 16-49% of trades while its buys and sells cancel (7-12% directional vs 94% for the control). It inflates the trade count, not the dollars (6.4%).
  3. It is the venue, not the token. The same five xStocks are washed on Gate (clip 17-49%) but organic on Bybit (2-6%). Same token, issuer and underlying; only the venue differs.
  4. It has a start date. HOODX was clean for three months; an elevated clip appeared Oct 2025 and crossed into clearly-washed territory from March 2026 as real volume faded. $0.95M manufactured on HOODX alone.
  5. Unreachable by chance. Resampling the organic controls (same-class and liquid), the top size never exceeds 8% in 3,000 draws; the flagged 16-49% sits far outside the null (p < 1/3000). Cheap to run, which is why it is widespread.
